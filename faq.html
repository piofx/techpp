GENERAL FAQ

What is covered in Java Fullstack Developer Training? + −

Concepts of Core Java, Advanced Java, Spring framework, and AWS.

Why should you join us ? + −

1. We train you on Fullstack development

2. We provide placements

3. We provide soft skills training

4. We give personal guidence

How long you can attend the placement drives? + −

We will be providing a minimum of 10 company drives. However you can atttend the drives upto one year of joining the program.

How many classes will be provided ? + −

There will be 160 hours of training, Spanning over 80 days and 2 hours a day.

How much does the programme cost ? + −

The programme costs 30,000 and material cost 3000, however you will be provided Scholarship depending on you pre-assessment score.

What if I get hired immediately after joining the course, will my money get refunded ? + −

The money will not get refunded as you can continue the training with us, to get better in your skills.

What are the class timings ? + −

The class timings are, during the evening first batch starts at 5pm.

Who are we ? + −

PacketPrep is the training vertical for xplore, where you get trained on the necessary skills to be placed in a good company.

How many drives are there in a month ? + −

As per the requirements of the companies. We will conduct the drives.

Why do you need to learn Fullstack development ? + −

As the demand for this role has increased and the future of the developers is really bright.

What did we do ? + −

In June last year we have organised the first virtual career fair in collaboration with Gurunanak College for 40 companies and we have closed 1500 positions since August 2020 - December 2020.

What if we don’t get placed in any company? + −

We will be sending you to the placement drives until 1 year of joining the course, if in case you do not get selected by any company we shall look for a different position and place you in a company.

Is it guaranteed that if we join we will definitely be placed? + −

We only grantee you that we will enhance your skills to the point where you will get selected in a very good company according to you own skills.

Yes, it is open for all the BTech students who are interested to enhance their skills .

No, anyone with a passion for learning the subject can take up the training. However, basic knowledge of a computer is required.
